Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti will always hold a very special place in my heart. It will always be my favorite dish that both my parents could ever make. I will never forget the way my mouth salivates when I smell the sweet aroma sweeping throught the house. I have had many spaghetti dishes from a wide variety of places through out my years on this earth yet this one will always be my favorite.

It is outrageously simple and even uses store bought spaghetti sauce because if it aint broke don't fix it, right? Another controversial piece of the recipe is the use of angel hair noodles instead of their thicker siblings. But to all the haters, poop you.

Ingredients

  • Prego Garlic and Herb sauce
  • Angel hair spaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb Ground Beef (of your choosing)
  • Garlic Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Place ground beef in a medium size pan. Place on stove and set it to a medium heat setting.
  2. Chop, mix, stir the ground beef until it is thoroughly chopped and browned. Make sure to sprinkle in some garlic powder and black pepper throughout this step.
  3. Start boiling a pot of water for the noodles when you are about half way done with step 2.
  4. Add all of the sauce once step two is complete and begin to mix and stir the sauce into the ground beef.
  5. Mix and then cover the pan. Mix every 4 minutes while you boil the noodles.
  6. Boil and strain the noodles.
  7. Once the meat and sauce dry out and don't look as watery, you are done.
  8. Plate the dish however you want and enjoy!:)
